FAQs to me

How do you like Colombia?

It's a beatiful country, I love the nature, it feels as if you're surrounded by mountains. By now the best views I've seen were in the department of Boyaca. Of course, I plan to see much more of this country. Oh, also the tropical fruits are delicious.

Is it cold in Russia?

Depends on the season and the climatic zone you're at. I lived for a long time in Siberia and surely the winters there are cold. At the same time they are incredibly beautiful and I miss them a lot. And in the hot zone in summer it can reach +40 and more. It's all relative.

What are the best dishes to eat in Ukraine?

Borsch. Salo. Sirniki. And much more. Go around its' capital, visit the restaurants of local cuisine. Believe me, you won't regret it.
